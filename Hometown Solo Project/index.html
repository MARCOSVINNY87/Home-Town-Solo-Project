Top three activities to do in Rio de Janeiro

Discover Rocinha Up Close

Explore Brazil’s largest favela on safe, guided tours filled with culture, street art, and powerful stories of community and resilience — the true spirit of Rio.

Feel the Brazilian Pride

The people’s joy is the heart of Brazil! Experience the vibrant energy of local celebrations, colors, and warmth that will make you feel right at home.

Ride to the Top of Sugarloaf

Take the cable car and enjoy one of the world’s most iconic views! From the top of Sugarloaf Mountain, the beauty of Rio unfolds before your eyes.
